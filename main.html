Be there

from December 1st to December 22nd, it’s “Discovery Month”

RoseUp is your online platform that offers you personalized and human support, to help you cope better with your cancer, from the moment you are diagnosed with the disease, during and after your treatments.

To end the year 2023,we have a surprise for you!From December 1st to 22nd, we invite you to discover our personalized support on RoseUp!

During this “Discovery Month”, you will have access to some of the webinars and workshops usually reserved for our members.

In the program :

  • Art therapy, socio-aesthetics, socio-hairdressing workshops…
  • Physiotherapy, reflexology, sophrology, meditation and many more webinars!
  • If you are affected by metastatic cancer: nutrition workshop and discussion time with a psychologist

If you like this support, you can fill out the form in the bottom take advantage of all the workshops and webinars that will be offered .

We look forward to seeing you on RoseUp event . To recive more about us, simply create your space in a few clicks to recive all our event in the futur.

Please don't forget to donate to breast cancer. You can help thousands by clicking on the "Donate" button at the top of the page and filling out the form.
